Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Overview

Restore Iran Flag is a browser extension that replaces the Iran flag emoji on Twitter/X with the official flag of Iran. This policy explains how the extension handles user data.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data.

The extension:

  • Does not collect personal information
  • Does not collect browsing history
  • Does not track user behavior
  • Does not use cookies
  • Does not send any data to external servers
  • Does not use analytics

How It Works

The extension runs entirely in your browser. It monitors Twitter/X pages for the Iran flag emoji and replaces the image with the official flag. All processing happens locally on your device.

Permissions

The extension requires access to Twitter/X domains (twitter.com, x.com, mobile.twitter.com) solely to detect and replace flag images. It does not access any other websites or data.

Third Parties

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above.
